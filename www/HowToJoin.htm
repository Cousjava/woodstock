Join Java.Net

The first thing to do to join Project Woodstock is to register on the Java.Net community site.

Join Project Woodstock

You can then join Project Woodstock. You can learn about different project roles. The roles for Project Woodstock are the same as other Java.Net projects.

New members will start as Observers. To see how to roles are defined by the project and how community members can change roles as their participation level increase, please read the Governance Policy document for Project Woodstock.

There are also email aliases for Project Woodstock. These are all open for all to join. The "dev" alias is where are project communication will be taking place, and is the best way to reach "developers" and propose new ideas or discuss problems or bugs.
